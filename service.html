Worship Service & Kids Ministry

What to Expect

Our service is designed to be straightforward and focused on the message of the Gospel. You can expect a 30-minute set of modern worship music and a 40-minute, expository sermon from Pastor John Smith.

We believe in coming as you are. The atmosphere is casual, and the teaching is rooted firmly in scripture. Childcare is provided for infants through 5th grade in our secure **'Shine Kids'** area during both services.
