Our Story & Beliefs

We are a non-denominational church founded on the principle of simplicity in faith and clarity in purpose. Established in 1955, our mission is to reflect the light of Christ in a complex world through focused worship and dedicated community service.

Our Core Mission

  • **Clarity:** To teach the Word of God with straightforward truth.
  • **Community:** To foster deep, authentic relationships among our members.
  • **Service:** To actively serve the city and promote justice and mercy.
